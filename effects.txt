EFFECTS:

Direct Effect
+- X of 1 or more stats
+- (spin) of 1 or more stats

Trade
-X of stat A, +Y of stat B
-X of stat, bonus (or no penalty) to movement

Move effect
+- X to next spin

Forced movement
Go X squares in Y direction

Restricted movement
Go only in X direction(s)

Warp
Go directly to another square

Warp choice
Go directly to one of X squares.

Battle bonus
Go directly to a battle with a bonus to your stats.


GAMBLING:

Battle
Spin higher than X - good/bad effect

Test
Stat higher than X - good/bad result
